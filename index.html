About Me

I am a dedicated set designer with a passion for creating environments that tell stories. My background in architecture and fine arts informs my approach to every project, blending structural integrity with artistic vision.

I believe that exceptional set design serves as a silent character in any production—shaping mood, directing attention, and enhancing narrative depth. My work has been recognized with multiple awards including the Art Directors Guild Award and Set Designer of the Year.

When I'm not designing sets, I'm exploring museums, sketching architectural details, or traveling to gather inspiration from diverse cultures and landscapes.
